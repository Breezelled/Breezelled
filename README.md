## <div align="center">Hi! 👋 I'm Breeze</div>  
  

I'm a PhD student at CRUISE Research Group, UNSW Sydney, advised by Prof. Flora Salim and Dr. Benjamin Tag. I'm also a visiting student researcher at Tsinghua University, collaborating with A/Prof. Yuntao Wang, and a research student member of the ARC Centre of Excellence for Automated Decision-Making and Society (ADM+S). I received my Master's degree from UNSW Sydney, advised by Prof. Flora Salim, Dr. Hao Xue, and Prof. Daniel Angus.

My research focuses on multimodal learning and intelligence for wearable systems and interaction. My broader research interests lie in human-centric AI, including self-supervised learning, generative AI, behavior modeling, human–AI interaction, and ubiquitous computing.
